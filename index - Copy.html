VALLAMAI FOUNDATION

Educate, Empower, Resurge

Objective of the Trust:

Improving the lives of children, adolescents and adults with neurological, rehabilitative and developmental needs through inpatient and day hospital programs, outpatient clinics, home and community services, education, and research. Improving the mobility of children and adults with neurological and rehabilitation needs. Facilitating support Parkinson’s, Stroke, Spinal Cord Injury. Autism, Cerebral Palsy Improving the accessibility solutions in the community for children, adolescent and adults with limited mobility. Improving and empowering the community Knowledge base and readiness to deal with emergencies , in the form of teaching in Schools, community programs about Basic Life Support Skills, enabling them to be Empowered true Citizens of India.Helping to be a proactive advocate for disability rights in Tamil Nadu.
