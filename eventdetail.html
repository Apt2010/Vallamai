International Conference on

Basic to future on Management
of Spinal cord Injury

Event on: December 16,2023 in Hotel Savera, Chennai.

We cordially invite you all, to this study day of Basics to future in SCI. This is part of the Vallamai trust foundation initiative, in quest of innovative and excellent technologies, to finda solution and cure for people affected with SCI and other chronic neurological problems. With knowledge sharing we can achieve excellence, perfection and high quality of care. We have emminent speakers with in-depth knowledge & expertise in the treatment of Spinal Cord Injuries. We are hoping to bridge the knowledge gap and build awareness of the latest technologies available to treat patient with spinal cord injuries.

About the Conference

This conference is unique in having Neurosurgeons,Neuro Physicians Rehabilitation Specialists, Psychiatrists, Physiotherapists, Occupational therapists, who are all involved in treating patients with SCI and giving them the best possible chance to recover and live life to the fullest.Join in,Register and be part of Research and Innovation in SCI.

Who can attend this conference?

Health care professionals interested in learning about Management of Spinal Cord Injury,Physical Medicine and Rehabilitation Trainees/Consultants,Neurology Trainees,Medical Interns and Residents,Physiotherapists, Physicians and Occupational Therapists.
